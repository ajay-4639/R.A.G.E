ACME Corp Company Policies

1. RETURN POLICY
All products purchased from ACME Corp may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Electronics must be returned within 15 days and must include all original packaging and accessories.
Custom-made products are non-refundable.
Shipping costs for returns are covered by ACME Corp for defective items only.

2. WARRANTY POLICY
All electronic products come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty.
Furniture items have a 5-year structural warranty.
Software products include 1 year of free updates and support.
Extended warranty plans are available for purchase at checkout.

3. EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year.
Sick leave is provided at 10 days per year and does not roll over.
Parental leave is 12 weeks paid for primary caregivers and 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.
Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

4. DATA PRIVACY POLICY
Customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
Personal information is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.
Users can request deletion of their data at any time through the privacy portal.
All data processing complies with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
Data retention period is 3 years from last interaction, after which data is automatically purged.

5. SHIPPING POLICY
Standard shipping is free for orders over $50.
Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99.
Overnight shipping is available for $24.99.
International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.
Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same business day.
